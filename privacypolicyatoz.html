Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

A to Z ABC Writing for Kids ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and users. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles user information.

1. Target Audience

This app is designed for preschool children to help them learn how to write English alphabets. We comply with applicable privacy regulations including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from users.

Specifically, the app does NOT collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Photos or media files
  • Device identifiers
  • Any personally identifiable information

3. How the App Functions

The app provides alphabet writing demonstrations and allows children to practice writing letters directly on the device. The app analyzes the written alphabets locally on the device to provide feedback on correctness. This process happens entirely on the device and no data is transmitted or stored externally.

4. Internet Usage

This app does not require an internet connection to function and does not transmit user data to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services such as advertising networks, analytics tools, or external data collection services.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, user privacy is fully protected. All alphabet writing analysis happens locally on the user's device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
